Samsung Galaxy Note 10 Specification

6.3-inch display

After the release of the Samsung Galaxy Note 9, most of the phones including the Samsung Galaxy S10 and S10+ that were released after that showed up with really impressive screen-to-body ratio and really thinner bezels. For those of you who were not happy with the fat bezels of the Galaxy Note 9, the new Samsung Galaxy 10 comes with a gorgeous 6.3-inch Dynamic AMOLED display with a whopping 90.9% screen-to-body ratio. One of the downsides of this one Samsung Galaxy Note 10 Price Dubaicompared to its predecessor is its resolution which is now 2280 x 1080 pixels with 19:9 aspect ratio instead of 2960 x 1440 pixels. Unlike the S series, the Galaxy 10 placed the punch-hole cutout in the upper middle side of the display. There are some visible changes on the back as the fingerprint scanner has moved under the screen. The camera setup now looks similar to the Samsung Galaxy A50. There are also some attractive colors to look forward to after the Samsung Galaxy Note 10 release date UAE: Aura Black, Aura Glow and Aura White

Triple Rear Camera Setup

One of the things that the Samsung Galaxy Note 9 was missing was an ultrawide camera and this time, we get a setup of a 27mm wide 12-megapixel sensor with f/1.5-1.8-2.4 aperture, a 12-megapixel 52mm telephoto sensor with f/2.1 aperture and a 16-megapixel 12mm ultrawide sensor with f/2.2 aperture. Once the phone is here after the Samsung Galaxy Note 10 release date UAE, we will be able to find out how it competes with the likes of Nokia 9 Pureview and OnePlus 7 Pro. Users will also get to enjoy new camera focused features like an improved Super Steady mode, Life focus video for bokeh and an advanced video editor. As for the front camera, it’s a 10-megapixel 26mm wide sensor which is slightly disappointing as it’s not too Samsung Galaxy Note 10 Dubai Pricewide.

3500mAh Non-removable Li-Ion with Reverse Wireless Charging Support

Surprisingly the battery of the Samsung Galaxy Note 10 took a bump and is now a slightly smaller 3500mAh rather than a 4000mAh one. However, users will be able to charge it faster thanks to 25W fast battery charging support which means even faster charging. This latest addition to the Galaxy Note series will also support reverse wireless charging and fast wireless charging. Unfortunately, there is no microSD card slot or 3.5mm headphone jack in the Note 10. There are some new S Pen focused features as well as this new S Pen now includes gyroscope and accelerometer. Users will be able to use the S Pen as almost like a wand for controlling their phones as it can work as a motion controller now. It’s slightly smaller than the older S Pens thanks to its more ergonomic design. As for the things under the hood, it’s a new Qualcomm Snapdragon 855 7nm processor or Exynos 9825 depending on your location and these processors will be accompanied by 8GB of RAM and 256GB built-in storage. Some of the other notable features are: Dolby Atmos/AKG sound, stereo speakers, Bluetooth 5.0, USB Power Delivery 3.0, improved Samsung DeX experience and USB 3.1, Type-C 1.0 port.

Things we loved about the Samsung Galaxy Note 10:
  • Thinner phone with higher screen-to-body ratio compared to its predecessor
  • Improved rear camera setup that includes an ultrawide camera
  • 25W fast battery charging and fast wireless charging
Things we didn’t like about this phone:
  • No 3.5mm headphone jack
  • The front camera is not really wide and the screen resolution is also lower
